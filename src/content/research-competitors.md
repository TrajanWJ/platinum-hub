# Competitors — Custom Plates & Plate Flippers

Last updated: 2026-06-19
Source: public web search (June 2026). Direct = same product space; Adjacent = related/marketplace.

## Direct — UK custom / show number plates
- **4D Platinum Customz** — https://4dplatinumcustomz.com/ — Peterborough, UK. 3D/4D + traditional
  show plates. Closest name + product overlap. **Watch this one.**
- **Numero Plates** — UK number-plate maker (press coverage 2023). Custom number plates.
- **platinumplates.co.uk** — UK private/registration number plates reseller. **Name collision** with
  your brand (SEO/handle confusion risk). Not you.
- **@platinumplatesuk** / **@platinumplates.co** — separate UK accounts, same name space.

## Direct — license plate flippers (motorized / display)
- **PlateVanish** — https://platevanish.com/collections/stealth-plate-flippers — stealth/motorized
  plate flipper kits (car meets, track, photos).
- **Stealthplate** — https://www.pinterest.com/stealthplate/ — one-button motorized plate flipper.
- **Relentless Off-Road Fabrication** — https://www.relentlessfabrication.com/products/flip-up-license-plate-mount
  — flip-up plate mounts.

## Adjacent — marketplaces (where buyers also shop)
- **Etsy** — plate flippers, license-plate flippers, motorcycle plate flippers, personalized
  decorative plates, wall displays for license plates. High volume of small custom sellers.
- **eBay** — "plate flip" listings.
- **Amazon** — license plate decor.
- **oplates** (TikTok Shop) — custom plates for motorcycles.

## Notes / takeaways
- Two distinct "plate flipper" meanings: (a) **motorized license-plate flippers** for vehicles
  (PlateVanish/Stealthplate), (b) **decorative wall-mounted display flippers**. Clarify which you make
  so the competitor set is right — current list covers both.
- Name collision risk: "4D Platinum Customz" and "Platinum Plates UK" are close to your brand. Worth
  checking SEO/handle confusion.
